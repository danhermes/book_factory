1. Writing & Editing
**The Hemingway Rewrite** Rewrite this email like Ernest Hemingway just slammed espresso and had to fire someone politely.
**Too Much Jargon?** Highlight every buzzword in this memo and then rewrite it like you’re explaining it to your neighbor’s dog.
**Rhyme It Out** Rewrite this Slack update as a short rhyming poem—but keep it accurate.
**Write Like a Pirate** Rewrite this status report as if it’s coming from the captain of a productivity galleon.
**What’s the sassiest professional way to say this?**

2. Meeting Support
**Meeting Haiku** Summarize this 45-minute meeting in a haiku. No seriously. Haiku.
**Boardroom Time Traveler** Translate these meeting minutes as if they were recorded in 1923 by a very confused stenographer.
**Stakeholder Improv** Simulate this meeting from the perspective of a stressed-out raccoon in a manager’s blazer.
**What’s the least boring way to recap this call in a team Slack channel?**
**Summon the Oracle** If a wise but cryptic oracle were to summarize this meeting, what would they say?

3. Content Creation & Repurposing
**Tagline or T-Shirt?** Turn this product description into a tagline, a tweet, and a T-shirt slogan.
**Rewrite by Zodiac** Rewrite this paragraph like a Sagittarius who just read their horoscope and is feeling bold.
**Game Show Host Voice** Pitch this content like a 1980s game show host with too much coffee.
**Alternate Universe Rewrite** Rewrite this onboarding message like the company is run by wizards.
**What if this explainer video script were written for an alien civilization with no concept of capitalism?**

4. Research & Document Analysis
**Detective Mode** Pretend you're a noir detective reading this policy. What’s suspicious, and who benefits?**
**Lawyer vs. Pirate** Explain this contract clause as both a corporate lawyer and then as a pirate.
**Summarize Like You’re 12** Explain this research paper like you're 12 and more interested in snacks than citations.
**What secret messages might this whitepaper be hiding if it were a coded manifesto?**
**Fortune Cookie It** Boil down this competitor analysis into three fortune-cookie-sized insights.

5. Strategic Planning
**Evil Genius Strategy** How would a cartoon villain plan to roll this out? Add a step-by-step plan with flair.
**OKR Horoscope** Rewrite these OKRs as astrological predictions, but make them strategic.
**Boardroom Musical** Pitch this strategic plan as a Broadway show synopsis. Include a musical number title.
**If Sun Tzu had to pitch this roadmap to a SaaS team, what would he say?**
**Draw Me a Dragon** If this strategy were a fantasy beast, what would it be—and why? Give me tactics to match.
