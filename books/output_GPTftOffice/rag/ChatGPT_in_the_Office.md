ChatGPT in the Office

1. Write
- Draft emails, reports, presentations
- Fix grammar, improve tone and clarity
- Adapt writing for audience and channel

2. Meet
- Summarize agendas, notes, and transcripts
- Generate action items and follow-up emails
- Role-play scenarios for prep (interviews, pitches)

3. Change
- Change one format to many (deck → blog → email)
- Brainstorm taglines, headlines, and social content
- Create outreach scripts and onboarding material

4. Research
- TL;DR long PDFs, whitepapers, and policies
- Extract insights, risks, and trends
- Compare documents or competitors side by side

5. Plan, Strategize, and Decide
- Generate decision matrices, SWOTs, business cases
- Break down high-level goals into tactical plans
- Explore "what if" scenarios with structured reasoning

6. Produce and Flow
- Build checklists, SOPs, templates
- Convert formats (CSV ↔ JSON ↔ Markdown)
- Draft logic flows for automation tools

7. Learn
- Teach tools, topics, or frameworks fast
- Explain complex ideas simply
- Prep for interviews, tests, or new roles

8. Communicate
- Write praise, constructive feedback, or tricky messages
- Mediate conflicts with diplomacy
- Match tone to audience (peer, exec, client)

9. Understand
- Summarize reviews, surveys, or social chatter
- Identify patterns, pain points, and priorities
- Suggest improvements based on tone/sentiment

10. AI Transformation
- Create prompt libraries for your team
- Build GPT workflows (assistants, bots, internal tools)
- Automate document generation, reports, and dashboards
