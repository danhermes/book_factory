### Messages that Miss the Mark
**Section Type: Net Zero Prompt**

One common pitfall in using ChatGPT for communication is constructing ineffective prompts that lead to subpar messages. Here are examples of such ineffective prompts and ways to refine them:

Terrible Prompt Example:

```
"Make my customer email better."
```

Without clarity or detail, such a prompt can pave the way for vagueness.

Better Prompt:

```
"Improve the following customer email by incorporating a friendly tone, clarifying our service benefits, and addressing the customer's concerns about pricing."
```

This revised prompt guides ChatGPT towards focused improvements, ensuring that the message’s intent doesn’t miss its mark. The key is specificity and providing context, thereby equipping ChatGPT to enhance communication purposively.
