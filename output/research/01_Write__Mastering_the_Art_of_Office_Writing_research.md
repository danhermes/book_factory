### Grammar Glitches

**Section Type: Net Zero Prompt**

Grammar errors have long been the bane of professional writing. However, the transition to AI-assisted writing has occasionally introduced its own quirks. Recognizing AI's limitations, we learn vital lessons in prompt crafting to avoid grammatical mishaps.

A renown example of a flawed prompt might look similar to this:

```

```

The issue here is the lack of specificity. Using vague terminology prompts ChatGPT to misinterpret the intended tone or content structure, leading to errors. To combat this, it's crucial to define clear, context-rich prompts, such as:

```
"Craft a response to the attached email, highlighting key points with clear bullet points and maintaining a formal and courteous tone."
```

Purposeful instructions prevent miscommunication and streamline the process, ensuring AI-driven grammar assists rather than detracts.
